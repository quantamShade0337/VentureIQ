Common questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before committing.

Is there a free trial?
+
Yes. Pro and Elite plans come with a 7-day free trial — no credit card required to start. You'll only be charged if you choose to continue after the trial period ends.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees and no lock-in periods. Your access continues until the end of your current billing cycle.
Do you offer refunds?
+
We offer a full refund within 7 days of your first charge if you're not satisfied with RizelIQ. After 7 days, refunds are handled case-by-case — reach out to our support team and we'll make it right.
Is my data secure?
+
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3. We never sell your data to third parties. Your scan history and saved companies are private to your account only.
Can I switch plans?
+
Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll be billed a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal.
What counts as a "scan"?
+
A scan is any time you submit an idea to the Idea Scanner and a full intelligence report is generated. Re-running the same idea counts as a new scan. Browsing saved reports or using search does not count as a scan.
